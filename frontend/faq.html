Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for orders, delivery, sizing, returns, payment, and customer care.

Orders are processed quickly after payment confirmation. Delivery timelines depend on your location and are shared during checkout or by support after dispatch.

Use your account order history or contact support with your order number. Tracking details are sent once your order ships.

Eligible items can be returned or exchanged within the stated return window when unworn, unwashed, and in original condition.

Omanutro supports secure checkout options using M-Pesa for now.

Use the dedicated Size Guide for chest, shoulder, sleeve, length, waist, and international size comparisons.

Some Omanutro pieces are intentionally cut wider with dropped shoulders. Check each product description and compare your measurements before ordering.

Contact support as soon as possible. Changes can only be made before the order has been packed or dispatched.
