Fit Guide

Size Guide

Measure once, style with confidence. Use these references for men's, women's, and oversized Omanutro pieces.

How to Measure

Chest
Measure around the fullest part of your chest with the tape flat.
Shoulder
Measure from shoulder seam to shoulder seam across the back.
Length
Measure from the highest shoulder point down to the hem.
Sleeve Length
Measure from shoulder seam to cuff with the arm relaxed.
Waist
Measure around your natural waist without pulling the tape tight.

Oversized Fit

Omanutro oversized pieces are designed with extra room through the chest and body, a relaxed shoulder drop, and a longer streetwear drape. Choose your normal size for the intended oversized look, or size down for a cleaner fit.
