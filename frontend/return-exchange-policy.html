Return & Exchange Policy

We want every OMANUTRO piece to feel right. This policy explains eligible returns, exchanges, timelines, item condition, and refund handling.

Return Window

Eligible items may be returned or exchanged within 14 days of delivery. Requests received after this window may not be accepted unless required by applicable law or approved by customer support.

Eligible Items

Returned items must be in original condition so they can be reviewed properly.

  • Unworn, unwashed, and unused.
  • Original tags, labels, and packaging included where applicable.
  • No stains, odors, alterations, damage, or signs of extended wear.
  • Proof of purchase or order confirmation available.

Items Not Eligible

Some items cannot be returned or exchanged for hygiene, personalization, final sale, or condition reasons.

  • Final sale, clearance, gift card, or custom items unless faulty.
  • Items returned damaged, worn, washed, altered, or without required tags.
  • Returns sent without contacting support first.

How to Start a Return or Exchange

Contact support.omanutro@gmail.com with your order number, item details, reason for return or exchange, and clear photos if the item is defective or incorrect.

Our team will review the request and provide next steps. Do not ship items back before receiving instructions from OMANUTRO support.

Exchanges

Exchanges depend on product availability. If the requested size, color, or item is unavailable, we may offer an alternative, store credit, or a refund where eligible.

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed after the returned item is received and inspected. Refund timing depends on the payment method and provider. Original delivery fees may be non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a confirmed fault.

Shipping Costs

Customers may be responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived faulty, damaged, or incorrect. We recommend using a trackable delivery method when returning items.
