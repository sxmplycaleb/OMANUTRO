What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on your device by your browser. Similar technologies, such as local storage and pixels, may also be used to remember choices and understand website activity.

Types We Use

Essential Cookies

These support core functions such as cart behavior, account sessions, checkout, security, currency preferences, and theme preferences.

Performance Cookies

These help us understand page performance, errors, and how visitors move through the website so we can improve speed and usability.

Marketing and Experience Cookies

Where enabled, these may help measure campaign performance, remember preferences, or make product discovery more relevant.

Third-Party Tools

Some cookies or similar technologies may be provided by third-party services used for hosting, analytics, payments, social links, embedded tools, fraud prevention, or customer support.

Those providers may process data according to their own policies when you interact with their services.

Cookie Controls

You can adjust cookie settings in your browser. Blocking cookies may affect features such as cart persistence, sign-in, checkout, currency selection, and theme preference.

  • Use your browser settings to delete or block cookies.
  • Use private browsing modes when you do not want long-term local storage.
  • Review the Privacy Policy for broader information handling details.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy when our store features, analytics setup, or service providers change. The Last Updated date shows when this page was most recently revised.
