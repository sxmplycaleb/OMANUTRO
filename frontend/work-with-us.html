FAQ

Applicant questions.

Do I need professional experience?

Experience helps, but strong taste, reliability, and a clear portfolio can matter just as much for creative roles.

Can I apply for more than one role?

Yes. Choose the closest position in the form and mention the other areas you can support in your cover letter.

What should I include in my portfolio?

Send work that shows your current level clearly: campaigns, designs, shoots, videos, social posts, customer work, or relevant case studies.

Are remote applicants considered?

Some creative and marketing roles can be remote or hybrid. Production, modeling, and shoot-based roles may require local availability.

When will I hear back?

Shortlisted applicants are contacted after the team reviews fit, portfolio strength, and current role priorities.
