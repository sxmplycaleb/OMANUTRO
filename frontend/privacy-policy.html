Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how OMANUTRO collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you browse our website, create an account, place an order, contact support, or interact with our brand online.

This page is intended to give customers a clear operational summary. It does not replace any privacy rights that apply under local law.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, information created during your shopping session, and limited technical data needed to operate the store.

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Account, order, wishlist, cart, return, and customer support details.
  • Payment confirmation details from secure payment providers. We do not intentionally store full card numbers.
  • Device, browser, IP address, cookie, and usage data that helps us keep the website reliable and secure.

How We Use Information

We use customer information to provide the store experience, process orders, communicate with you, improve our services, and protect the website from misuse.

  • Process purchases, delivery, exchanges, refunds, and customer care requests.
  • Send transactional messages such as order confirmations, updates, and support replies.
  • Personalize product discovery, maintain carts, and improve website performance.
  • Detect fraud, secure accounts, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our terms.

Sharing and Service Providers

We may share only the information needed with trusted service providers who help us run the store, such as payment processors, delivery partners, hosting providers, analytics tools, and customer communication services.

We do not sell your personal information. Where required, service providers are expected to use information only for the service they provide to OMANUTRO.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect customer information. No online system is completely risk free, so customers should also use strong passwords and avoid sharing account access.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal information, subject to identity verification and any legal or operational retention requirements.

You can also manage browser cookies through your browser settings. For more details, read our Cookie Policy.

Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact us at support.omanutro@gmail.com or through the Contact page.
